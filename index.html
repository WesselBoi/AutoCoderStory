ABHIMANYU

The Heroic Sacrifice in the Chakravyuh

Episode 1

The Call to Duty

Abhimanyu, in the absence of Arjuna, is called upon by the Pandavas to take the lead in breaking the Chakravyuh formation.

Episode 2

Entering the Chakravyuh

Abhimanyu, armed with partial knowledge of the Chakravyuh strategy, bravely breaches the outer layers of the deadly formation.

Episode 3

Warriors Fall Behind

Despite initial success, the Pandava warriors like Bhima and Yudhishthira fail to follow Abhimanyu into the Chakravyuh, leaving him alone inside.

Episode 4

The Inner Circle

Abhimanyu battles fiercely as he breaks through deeper layers of the Chakravyuh, demonstrating his prowess as a warrior.

Episode 5

The Trap Tightens

The Kaurava commanders, led by Dronacharya, form an impenetrable circle around Abhimanyu, preparing to overwhelm him with superior numbers.

Episode 6

Facing Karna

Abhimanyu faces off against Karna, one of the deadliest warriors in the Kaurava army, in a crucial encounter.

Episode 7

Drona's Deception

Abhimanyu fights valiantly, but Dronacharya's strategy and manipulation of the formation lead to Abhimanyu's further isolation.

Episode 8

The Battle Turns Unfair

The Kaurava warriors break the rules of war, attacking the young hero all at once, a violation of dharma in battle.

Episode 9

The Final Stand

Abhimanyu, despite being gravely outnumbered, fights until his last breath, using everything in his arsenal, from his bow to chariot wheels, as weapons.

Episode 10

The Aftermath of Tragedy

The Pandavas mourn the loss of Abhimanyu, who died a hero's death, and resolve to avenge the unfair tactics used by the Kauravas.
